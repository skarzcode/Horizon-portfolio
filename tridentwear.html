About OasisFits

Design

TridentWear is another store with a large catalogue and this theme has a variety of methods to showcase them. I used complimenting colours throughout the store to have a simple yet clean store. Images were displayed in landscape orientation to make use of the space and engage consumers.

Development

When developing the store i wanted to utilise all the space available while have a clean store. I structed the different method of collection in manner that would make the store-front engaging and elegant. The more prominent product/collection were displayed with engaging effects such as hotspot and scrolls.

Favourite Part

This theme offers "Hotspot" effect which is a effective way to demonstrate your best selling products and it enables to you layer multiple products in one image increasing sales and giving the consumer a better user experience. My second favourite part will have to be the mega menu for its ability to show collections in the navigation with an image instead of having a boring static bar on the top.
