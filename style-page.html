TriStudio

Utilizing the premium theme, Highlight, I created a simple yet elegant eCommerce site for an arts and painting brand, TriStudio. This project showcases store set-up and advanced features like parallax-scroll effects, configuration and the Strategy and implementation behind a modern and professional looking store.

About TriStudio

Design

This was a arts and painting store with minimal products. My design plan was to engage and tell as story utilising the themes vertical scroll feature. Aiming to give a seamlessly easy Â experience by displaying the collection and not cramming the store with to much content. Less is more. I specialise in simplistic yet eye catching results.

Development

My plan and strategy for this store was not only to tell a story but to also make sure every piece of content compliment and matched each other. Whether it be the background colour to the logo or the fonts and text colour to the products.

Favourite Part

My two favourite part will have to be the vertical scroll for its simplicity yet elegant touch and the parallax scroll for its minimal animation and the way it layers itself complimenting the vertical scroll.
